Director’s Message
“Success comes to those who work hard and Stays with those who don’t rest on the past,” Education is not merely acquirement of facts but also of values which help us improve the different faces of mankind. It ensures that we leave the world a far better place than we found it.
We enterprise to map academic excellence with a good human value system, which in quality ways is ingrained in the culture of the school. To achieve this, we strongly feel that a paradigm shift in the minds of all stake holders should take place so that when the school becomes the hub to initiate and formalize education it has its tenets home where it is further strengthened. our commitment to such education will always persist in all our endeavour.
Academic excellence with character and personality development is our ultimate goal. The well chalked out academic and co-curricular programmes enables the students to attain their full potential to develop life skills. The academic work is centered on highly personalized attention and supervision.
Every child is like a seed which has the potential to grow if nourished with love and care under the right guidance. Childhood is the best time to lay the foundation of the core values essential for the overall development of the future citizen of tomorrow. Our mission is to provide positive and all-round motor development to every CHILD.
At DUV International School, the calibre and talent of each child is recognized and nurtured. We emphasis on LEARNING and not on teaching. Education is a complete process that leads to the attainment of the full potential of the child. Equal attention is given to the development of the children both academically and in co-curricular activities like Sports, yoga, cultural activities, etc.
Education is not just about the subjects that are learnt and taught in school. The urge for knowledge is not restricted to the child. It is also essential for a parent and a teacher to keep adding to their own knowledge bank by keeping their eyes and ears open and by reading as much as possible and ensuring that children develop these habits as well.
Our focus is to develop our students as global citizens, with tolerance, respect and-appreciation of diverse cultures and religions for a life-time learning experience.
At DUV International School we are dedicated to exhaustive quality learning and are utterly committed to a continuous process of improvement for the benefit of the students.

Our vision at DUV International School is to encourage our students to think, take decisions independently and empowered to acquire value knowledge and skills  to become  capable, responsible, and dedicated towards a tradition of excellence in this world.  full of knowledge, confidence cum passion and cheer and to develop tolerance  to lead a happy, meaningful, balanced & graceful life contributing towards progress, prosperity and peace in the world.
DUV International School is committed to provide a stable, caring and supportive family like environment, where students can receive mature guidance through daily interaction with able faculty and become accountable, productive, and ethical citizens with the skill to think innovatively, reason critically, communicate effectively, and respect peoples of other cultures. DUV International School is also eyeing upon providing such a resourceful learning environment where students can develop into “Citizen of the World” and to develop the learning through differentiated, quality based with a commitment to development and quality teaching.To empower teachers to guide children with means to construct knowledge. To bring back an element of joy to school surrounding which would nudge the child to grow into full bloom. and children be responsible citizen to face challenges of future.
