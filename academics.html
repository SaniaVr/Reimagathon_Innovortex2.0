Pre-Primary Curriculum
The Child Centric Curriculum in Pre – Primary is in accordance with Play-way method with activity- based learning. The curriculum is designed to cater the individual child’s potential and interests. Activities like Painting, Singing, Dancing, Moral & Values, Sports, Story Telling, Karate and various Competitions and celebrations provide ample of opportunities to develop and showcase their creativity. A variety of hand on activities promote first hand experiences, useful for learning. We at the Primary Wing believe that the mind, like a parachute works best when opened. Keeping this initiative in mind, the scholastic and co-scholastic aspects of the curriculum and the evaluation procedure is developed for building the student’s knowledge, potential & talents, to the optimum level. Classes are colourful, joyfull, ventilated room, with friendly enviroment , and Theme based curriculum. The curriculum includes English, Hindi, Math, Enviromental Studies, Moral Values, Art and craft, General Knowledge , Computer Literacy Programme and co-curricular activities. Students pursue their hobbies and interests in class room activities. The following exposure are given to the students. 1. Morning Assemblies 2. English Club 3. Math Club 4. Computer Laboratories 5. Handicraft 6. Puppet Making 7. Painting, Sketching and Paper Folding 8. Rangoli & Floral Designing 9. Sports 10. Indoor & Outdoor Games 11. Story Telling 12. Debate Club A number of celebrations, school functions including Annual Concert, Annual School Social Science Exhibition, Annual School Science Exhibition, Child fair with parents, Annual school sports day, mathematics and Science Olympiads, Special Assemblies and various competitions provide ample opportunities to our students to develop and showcase their creativity and talent.
The curriculum is designed and packaged in a manner that challenges students to ask questions, enhance skills, conduct in-depth knowledge, research, analyze findings, build conceptual understanding, share their learning in variety of ways that reflect on their responsibility. Each child is supported to fulfill his/her unique potential with a balanced student friendly environment. 1. The middle wing of the school strives to maintain excellence in academics and co-curricular activities to enable the students to stand at par in today’s competitive world. 2. We design the curriculum broadly on the guidelines issued by Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) from time to time. 3. The curriculum is designed in conformity with Educational Board’s guidelines wherein C.C.E Pattern of Evaluation is followed which comprise of: Scholastic:- English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, AI(Artificial intelligence),Moral Values, General Knowledge, Art & Craft. Apart from regular text books in English, an additional supplement reader is added books to inculcate reading habits. Co-Scholastic:- Life skills, Environment Awareness, Value Education, Performing Art, Visual Arts and various competition (which include drawing, colouring, sketching, painting, sculpture and craft work.) Health & Physical Education, (which includes physical exercises, athletics, yoga and meditation) and Sports (which includes cricket, football, kho-kho and indoor games) Beside this other many curricular activities are conducted including class debates, Essay writing and Inter-School Competition. Excursions cum Educational tour (local and out station) are also taken up by school from time to time.
